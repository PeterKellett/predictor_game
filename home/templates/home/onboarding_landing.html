Gameplay and Rules

This game comprises of two parts, a pre-world cup wizard and a daily score predictor.

  • In the pre-world cup wizard you try to forecast each teams progress throughout the tournament and must be completed before the tournament kicks off.
  • In the daily score predictor you try to predict the scores of each match and is played each day as the tournament progresses.

Points

Golden Route
  • Group stage - 1 point awarded for each correct result
  • Knockout rounds - 1 point awarded for each team that you forecast to be in that round and an extra point awarded if they are in the path indicated by the golden route.
  • Winner - 10 points awarded if you get the correct winner of the World Cup.
Silver Goal
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the home team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the correct number of goals the away team scores.
  • 1 point awarded if you get the match outcome correct.
  • Score a match hat-trick, ie, get all 3 points above and you score an extra point for getting the match ball.
Overall scoring
  • Your overall score is the total number of points scored across both games.
